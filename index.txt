:keywords:  finance, trading, domain specific languages, domain-specific languages, DSLs, programming, conference, workshop

The Domain-Specific Languages for Financial
Systems (DSLFIN) 2013 workshop was held on 1 October 2013 in
Miami, Florida in association with the
http://modelsconference.org[ACM/IEEE 16th International Conference on
Model Driven Engineering Languages and Systems (MODELS 2013)].

TIP: The workshop took place on 1 October 2013. You can find videos of
 the presentations below. We encourage you to visit the
 link:community.html[community page] to join the workshop mailing list
 and to be notified of upcoming financial DSL events. A comprehensive listing of
 financial DSLs resources can be found on the
 link:resources.html[resources page].
